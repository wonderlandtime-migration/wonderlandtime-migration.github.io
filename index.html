Wonderland Time

What is Wonderland?

The Wonderland Time project is a decentralized reserve currency protocol available on the Avalanche Network, backed by assets in the treasury. Its goal is to build a policy-controlled currency system, native on the AVAX network, which optimizes for stability and consistency so that TIME (Wonderland's currency token) can function as a global unit-of-account and medium of exchange. In order to achieve price stability without fiat money, Wonderland intends to optimize the system for growth and wealth creation. The short term intention is to detach from traditional finance world with cryptocurrency industry.

How can I use Wonderland and what are the benefits?

Wonderland is a protocol for staking TIME tokens on the Avalanche Blockchain. Wonderland was designed to give stability and security to the token supply, while giving an opportunity for those who stake their tokens to earn more of them. There are two groups that can benefit from Wonderland: Stakers and Minters. Stakers will benefit from supply growth as new TIME tokens are minted which they receive in reward; Minters will benefit from price consistency as they commit capital upfront at low risk, with fixed returns given in TIME tokens.
